About Mnomis

Mnomis was created to solve a persistent failure in healthcare outreach: care teams know what needs to happen, but often lack insight into how each member is most likely to receive outreach at the moment action is required.

Interpretation, not prediction

Mnomis does not simply predict what members will do. It interprets observed behavior to infer how they are most likely to receive outreach now. That interpretation is translated into operational guidance that care teams can act on immediately.

Built for real workflows

Outputs are designed for human interpretation, not black-box scoring. Every brief includes drivers, confidence, and escalation notes, supporting clinical review and compliance review.

What we aim to improve

  • Follow-through on care actions
  • Reduced wasted outreach effort
  • Higher response rates without changing clinical content
  • Clear guidance that care teams can trust
