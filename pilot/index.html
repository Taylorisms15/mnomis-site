A low-friction way to evaluate Mnomis

The pilot is an access mechanism. It lets you evaluate outreach guidance on your population using existing engagement history, without integration work.

See the Brief Request Pilot

Pilot overview

Input

A small export of engagement history and outreach touchpoints. No PHI is required for the pilot.

Output

Outreach Intelligence Briefs for a cohort plus a summary view for operational planning.

Measurement

A practical plan to compare response and follow-through with consistent clinical content.

What we need

  • Cohort definition and timeframe
  • Outreach events by channel and date
  • Engagement outcomes such as response, scheduled, completed, or no-show

We can start with a small sample, even 1,000 rows, to validate output structure and measurement.
